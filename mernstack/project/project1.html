Gulab Jamun


Ingredients :

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying

Recipe :

  1. Make Dough: Mix 1 cup milk powder, 1/4 cup maida, 1/2 tsp baking powder, 1 tbsp ghee, and 2-4 tbsp warm milk into a soft dough.

  2. Shape Balls: Divide dough into 14-16 small portions, rolling gently into smooth, crack-free balls to prevent breaking while frying.

  3. Fry Balls: Deep fry in oil or ghee on low-medium heat until deep golden brown, turning constantly for even cooking.

  4. Make Syrup & Soak: Boil 1 cup sugar, 1 cup water, and cardamom for 5 mins; soak hot fried balls in warm syrup for 1-2 hours.
